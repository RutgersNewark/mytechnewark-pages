End-of-semester workflow

Use this checklist to move from the last class meeting to a clean, reusable course shell.

Before last class
  • Confirm final assignment due dates and exam expectations.
  • Post a Canvas announcement with end-of-semester instructions.
  • Remind students how to ask final questions or request help.
  • Review missing, excused, or unresolved work before final grading begins.
Goal: reduce last-minute confusion before final work is due.
After last class
  • Grade remaining submissions and review SpeedGrader comments.
  • Check the Canvas gradebook for missing grades, hidden grades, and calculation issues.
  • Confirm final letter grades before using GradeSync or entering grades in REGIS.
Goal: make sure your grade records match the final grades you intend to submit.
Submit grades
  • Submit grades directly in REGIS, or use GradeSync from RU Faculty Tools when available and you have REGIS access.
  • If you do not have access to your course in REGIS, GradeSync will not work; contact your department for assistance.
  • Follow Registrar or departmental guidance for incompletes, pass/no credit, withdrawals, or other special grade situations.
  • Verify submitted grades before closing your records.
REGIS is the official system of record. Canvas grades are not official until submitted.
Archive and reuse
  • Export your Canvas gradebook and save any course records you need.
  • Export course content if you need a local backup.
  • Clean up outdated files, announcements, dates, links, and copied content.
  • Copy the course into the next term shell when it is available.
Most Canvas courses conclude automatically based on term settings. Check with ATS before manually concluding a course.
