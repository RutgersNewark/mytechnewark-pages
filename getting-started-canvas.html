Getting Started in Canvas

Canvas is the way you teach. Whether you prepare your syllabus before the semester begins or build and publish content gradually, Canvas gives you the flexibility to create meaningful learning experiences for your students — every step of the way.

Flexible Course Workflows

Canvas meets you where you are in the semester. Start with the basics — a syllabus and a few files — and add richer content, quizzes, and discussions as you go. You control what students see and when they see it.
