Why Faculty Use EchoPoll

Included with Canvas

No additional licensing cost. Available to all RU-N and RBS instructors through Canvas — no extra setup required.

No Additional Student Cost

Students respond using any web-enabled device. No app download or account required.

Supported by ATS

Training, consultations, and implementation support available through Academic Technology Services.

Getting Started with EchoPoll

Four steps to your first poll.

1

Access EchoPoll through Canvas

Go to your Canvas course and open EchoPoll from the course navigation menu.

2

Create Your First Activity

Choose a question type and set up your first poll or engagement activity.

3

Run Your Poll in Class

Launch the poll during class and display results in real time for students to see.

4

Review Results & Participation

View participation data and optionally sync grades to the Canvas Gradebook.
